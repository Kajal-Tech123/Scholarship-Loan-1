Central Government Scholarships

National Means Cum-Merit Scholarship (NMMSS)

For Class 9–12 students from families earning less than ₹3.5 lakh/year. Provides ₹12,000 per year aid.

Central Sector Scheme of Scholarship (CSSS)

For Class 12 toppers (top 20th percentile) in professional courses. Offers up to ₹20,000 per year for 4 years.

INSPIRE Scholarship

For top 1% Class 12 scorers in sciences. Provides ₹80,000 per year till PhD to encourage research careers.
